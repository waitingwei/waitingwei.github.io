This art describes a picture of Laozi’s Dao De Jing. The original excerpt is “Rarely people live in smaill contry. They don’t use utilities even they own it; they don’t go far away even they sick heavily; they don’t board even they have boat; they don’t war even they have armies. People back to original living way. Gorgeous goverment which is their food is plain and good, their clothesfine but simple, their homes secure; Though they live within sight of their neighbors, and crowing cocks and barking dogs are heard across the way, yet they leave each other in peace while they grow old and die.”
